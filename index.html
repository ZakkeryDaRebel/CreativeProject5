What is D&D?

Dungeons and Dragons (D&D), is a fun table top roleplaying game (TTRPG) that you can play with your friends. The game consists of a team of adventurers (that's you and your friends),comprised of different skills and abilities, embarking on a quest, given by the Dungeon Master (DM), to accomplish both great and small tasks. If you've never played D&D before, this website will help you understand how to get started. If you've already played D&D before, this website is good to share with your friends, but it will also come with some tips and tricks that you may find to be pretty handy and useful.

What you need to play

  • A basic understanding of the rules
  • A group of freinds to play with (One of which needs to be the DM)
  • A rulebook (Player's Handbook is the most helpful for players
  • A character (this can be made using the How to Create a Character page)
  • Dice, either real or online

What you can find on the Classes page?

Since choosing your class is the most important decision you'll make in Dungeons and Dragons, I have a page dedicated to just talking about Classes. Each Class has their own section that has a brief overview of the class as well as describing a Special Ability they have. It also includes what is the classes Primary Ability.

What you can you find on the Races page?

Races are still a vital part of your character, and it goes hand and hand with what class you choose. So on this page you'll find each race in the Player's Handbook with a brief overview on their culture and what they're like. Included is what kind of Ability increases each Race has.
